1 /**
2  * @file ntt.h
3  * @brief Number-Theoretic Transform (NTT) and Montgomery arithmetic.
4  *
5  * Provides NTT-based polynomial multiplication over prime fields for
6  * big-integer arithmetic. Three built-in NTT primes are included, all
7  * of the form p = c * 2^k + 1, supporting transforms up to 2^max_power_two
8  * elements. Chinese Remainder Theorem (CRT) reconstruction is provided to
9  * lift results back to a larger ring.
10  */
