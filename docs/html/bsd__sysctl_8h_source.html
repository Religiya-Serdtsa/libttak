7  * The project-wide -D_XOPEN_SOURCE=700 flag hides BSD-specific types

9  *
10  * NetBSD – devmajor_t, u_int, sysctl() prototype are gated on
11  * _NETBSD_SOURCE, which _XOPEN_SOURCE suppresses.
12  * OpenBSD – u_long and other BSD types are gated on __BSD_VISIBLE,
13  * which <sys/cdefs.h> forces to 0 when _XOPEN_SOURCE is set.

15  * Fix: temporarily remove _XOPEN_SOURCE while pulling in the sysctl
